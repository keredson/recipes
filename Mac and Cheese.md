Mac and Cheese
==============

This is the first homemade mac and cheese I've made whose texture I liked.  It's a very simple/basic flavor.  Kids liked it.  Could be a good base for a more complicated version.

Ingredients
-----------

- 1 lb pasta
- 1 can evaporated milk
- 2 tbsp butter
- 3 slices American cheese
- Enough cheddar to make 8 oz total cheese (including the american).
- 2 tbsp pecorino romano (or similar) cheese
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp yellow mustard
- Black pepper to taste (optional)

Directions
----------

1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain.
2. Add evaporated milk and butter, mix and wait for butter to melt.
3. Add American cheese, mix and wait for it to melt.
4. Add cheddar and romano and wait for it to melt.
5. Add salt and pepper.
