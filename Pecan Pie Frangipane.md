Pecan Pie Frangipane
========

Ingredients
-----

- 1lb pecans
- 1.5c brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2tsp vanilla extract
- 1 stick butter
- 1 pie crust
- 1/2tsp salt

Instructions 
----

1. toast the pecans
2. brown the butter
3. blend two thirds of the pecans in a food processor
4. add the sugar, eggs, salt and vanilla extract
5. stream in the butter
6. mix in the remaining pecans
7. add to pie crust
8. bake
